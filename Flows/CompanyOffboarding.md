# Company Offboarding

The company offboarding flow manages the process of removing a company from the system.

## Overview

Specific procedures for offboarding a company entity.

## Steps

1. **Instructions**: Review dependencies (Branches, Departments, Employees) that must be removed first.
2. **Delete Company Settings**: Remove configuration settings.
3. **Delete Company**: Final deletion of the company entity.
