# Company Onboarding

The company onboarding flow manages the process of introducing a new company to the system.

## Overview

Specific procedures for onboarding a company entity.

## Steps

1. **Create Company**: Basic company information (Name, Country, etc.).
2. **Create Company Settings**: Configure tenant ID, user ID, and color scheme.
3. **Upload Company License**: Upload the license file for the company.
4. **Create Branch**: Create the initial branch for the company.
